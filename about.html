Our Story

Hi there! I’m Ally, the heart and hands behind Dream Glory Co. What started as a weekend hobby making scrunchies for friends has blossomed into a small business dedicated to handcrafted accessories—and we’re so glad you’re here.

Every piece—from our cherry‑blossom scrunchies to our spellbinding wax melts—is designed, crafted, and packaged by me with love and attention to detail. I believe that the little things in life—the pop of color on your keys, the comforting glow of a scented melt—bring daily joy and allow you to express your unique style.

When I’m not in my home studio, you’ll find me enjoying time with my family, brainstorming new designs, or curled up with a good book (and yes—a sweet bookmark close at hand!). Thank you for supporting my dream—your order truly makes a difference.
